CACIO PEPE

SIMPLE AND DELICIOUS DONE IN MINUTES!

A dish prepared with few traditional Italian ingredients, simple and genuine, which has its roots in the world of sheep farming and quality raw materials.

A triad of simple but strong flavors that come together in this recipe, perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 350 g of Spaghetti
  • 220 g of Pecorino Romano
  • 14 g of whole black peppercorns
  • 1,750 liters of water
  • 1/2 tablespoon of coarse salt

Instructions:

  1. First start by grating the Pecorino Romano. When ready, set aside in a bowl.
  2. Toast the ground pepper in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir occasionally and check to make sure it does not burn.
  3. It will take 2 to 3 minutes and you will smell the intense aroma of the pepper.
  4. Now boil the water with very little coarse salt (maximum 1/2 tablespoon) to cook the pasta.
  5. When the water boils, put in the spaghetti. Let the pasta cook for about 5 minutes.
  6. Meanwhile add 2 ladles of pasta cooking water to the toasted black pepper and turn the heat under the skillet to low.
  7. While the pasta is cooking, make the Pecorino cream. Pour a ladleful of the starchy cooking water over the grated pecorino cheese and stir quickly. You will get a creamy consistency similar to ricotta.
  8. Once the spaghetti are cooked, turn off the heat, wait about half a minute and then add the pecorino cream a little at a time. Stir in the spaghetti evenly.
  9. If you like, add more grated pecorino just before serving and another sprinkle of pepper for garnish.
